Welcome to Formula 1 In Schools

What's Formula 1 In Schools

F1 in Schools is an international STEM challenge to design and manufacture a miniature car out of the official F1 Model Block using CAD/CAM design tools. The cars are powered by CO2 cartridges and are attached to a track by a nylon wire. They are timed from the moment they are launched to when they pass the finish line by a computer.

Chequered Flag Racing

My team is formed in 2015, and named as Chequered Flag Racing. It was renamed at July, 2016 with the new sponsor. After that, The team formally changed to LeSports Chequered Flag Racing. The Chequered Flag is a sign of the finish line. For every formula One race, Chequered Flag would wave for the winner, then Chequered Flag Racing contains our hope to be the fastest car and the best team
